Pre-Writing

Around the end of school and the start of summer in 2022, Pan Cubano the movie was a idea that was found as a small little joke but then kicked off as an actual, full movie in production.

First Script Idea

The First Ever Script Started May 29th of 2022 and ended writing on June 9th 2022. It was about 20 pages and was supposed to show ideas on how the movie plot would layout. It originally was going to be 3-D computer animated but then switched to live action.

Current Script

The Current script is being written and is about 26 pages in, It is planned to be at least 1 hour and 20 minutes in runtime on filming and has more ideas fit into its runtime. The Script will go through multiple drafts.

Current Productions

It is currently considered that the film will be shot on a 4k camera and will overdue many cameras and have many diffrent filming locations.

Planned Platforms

The Movie is planned to be ported on to Digital Media, DVD, and VHS. The films soundtrack will be planned to be on Digital, Cassette, CD, and Might be put on Vinyl Records.
